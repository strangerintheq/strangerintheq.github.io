Tentura is the fundamental device of time and being

The essence of the Tentura's work is that it breaks the connection between the body and soul of every living being under its influence. The subject becomes bound to a global variable, which is calculated and controlled by the Tentura itself.
To understand this, you can use the metaphor of virtual reality. A person starts a computer game and takes control of a character. The character acts under the influence of the player's will. In this case, the player personifies the character in its entirety - the soul (various game-mechanical characteristics) and the body (the image on the screen). The set of rules of the game symbolize the material world with its rigidly defined laws.
Imagine that at some point a third-party program turns off the player's means of input and begins to control the character itself, calculating their next actions according to some internal algorithm. In this case, the events taking place with the character are no longer determined by the will of the player or the rules of the game itself, but by a ready-made plot which was previously written by someone or generated by the program itself. This is the work of Tentura, with the amendment that the object of its control is reality.

About artwork

Tentura is divided in three different zones, which control the behaviour of strings or wires that belong to this zone. When a string leaves the zone where it started, it retains the color but changes its behaviour. String can stop when it changes zone, and if was not, it was changes main direction.
Starting spots of the strings have two different basic distribution rules - fully or partial distributed on the image. Partial distributions can be circle, ring, triangle, square, pentagon or lines, which can be shifted, rotated etc.

Creation process

This artwork is based on a noised vector field (also known as flow field), which can be 2 variations and 2 modifications:
After that, on top of this vector field drawn the one of 6 shapes with the "brush" effect (where field direction is changed with direction of brush moved):
These shapes also has variations, at first it can be shifted from center. Also it can be rotated or deformed. Sometimes shapes can be partial or spiral:
This three zones on vector field is used to control the behaviour of strings or wires, zones is providing not only directions but also color choose rules, turn chance and chance of stop.
Strings or wires distribution also different and can be one of this 6 types:
Distributions, except full, also has variations, it can be rotated, shifted, with hole, noised, and with any combination of this modifications:
Fully distributed wires can be with "cracks" made by several octaves of noise, other distributions can be "cut by edges" to prevent wires from crossing the edges.
All code written on typescript, and bundled to single javascript file with total size approximately 5.5 kilobytes.
