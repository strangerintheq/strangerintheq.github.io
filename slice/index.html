S L / C E

by Stranger in the Q

Long-form generative artwork released by artblocks.io
and hosted immutably on Ethereum blockchain

SL/CE (slice) is the exploration splitting

of primitive shapes into smaller parts

So primitive action - division into parts - we can found it everywhere, without parts there is no integrity, it is hard to determine what the whole is until it is divided into parts, sometimes we do not know what is inside - division helps here us to explore the subject, to understand what we are dealing with.
It is difficult to predict the result of the division of material objects, the division of simple initial forms makes the whole picture more complex, it is question - what will happen as a result of only 2 actions of random division.
.

Mint #0

About artwork

Slice is begins with selection of starter shapes. There are rectangles trapezoids or circles available. After the placement of starter shapes the splitting is used to determine final shapes. Sequence of slices is applied to shapes and forms the final composition.
